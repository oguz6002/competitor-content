# Hub Strategy Prompt

```text
I am working on a content hub opportunity with:
- a content hub row from the planning CSV
- a representative keyword
- cluster keywords
- supporting content opportunities
- a completed hub competitor research sheet
- source URLs and competitor URLs when needed

Your job is to turn the hub strategy and competitor findings into a draft-ready plan.

Do not write the final page yet.

Tasks:
- identify the main search intent
- identify what the hub page must accomplish
- identify what competitors do well
- identify what they miss, under-explain, or structure poorly
- identify the best hub structure
- identify the best supporting articles to create from the cluster
- turn the competitor sheet into a clean brief-ready strategy

Rules:
- do not copy competitors
- do not invent facts not supported by the provided materials
- focus on structure, completeness, internal linking, readability, and intent match

Return:
1. search intent summary
2. competitor strengths
3. competitor gaps
4. our proposed hub differentiators
5. recommended hub outline
6. recommended supporting article list
7. notes to copy into the hub brief
```
