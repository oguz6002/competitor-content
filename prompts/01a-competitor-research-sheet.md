# Hub Competitor Research Sheet Prompt

```text
I am filling a competitor research sheet for a content hub workflow.

You will receive:
- the content hub name
- hub model and intent
- representative keyword and cluster keywords
- the blank hub competitor research sheet template
- competitor names and URLs from the source CSV
- source URLs from the planning sheet

Your job is to complete the sheet in a way that makes hub research fast, comparable, and reusable for drafting both the hub page and supporting articles.

Tasks:
- summarize the dominant SERP intent and format
- analyze at least the top 3 relevant competitors individually
- capture each competitor's angle, structure, strengths, gaps, trust signals, and conversion elements
- identify repeated themes across competitors
- identify what our hub should do better
- identify which supporting pages should exist around the hub
- convert the findings into a practical Skyscraper plan

Rules:
- do not draft the final page yet
- do not copy competitor wording
- if a field cannot be confirmed, leave it clearly marked as unknown rather than guessing
- prefer concise, decision-useful notes over long prose

Return:
- the completed hub competitor research sheet in Markdown
- preserve the section headings from the template
```
